Formatting Manual:

  • YouTube Videos: Just paste a YouTube link (e.g. https://youtube.com/watch?v=...). It will automatically convert to a live playable video!
  • Images: Paste a direct link to an image ending in .jpg, .png, .gif and it will auto-embed. Or use Markdown: ![alt text](image_url).
  • Markdown & HTML: You can use standard Markdown for bold (**bold**), lists, and even standard HTML tags.
  • Indentation: Your spacing and indentation will be preserved perfectly.
