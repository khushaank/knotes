Guidelines

What to Submit

Anything that gratifies one's intellectual curiosity. Rare content is more valuable than popular content.

What not to Submit

Video, unless it contains a substantial amount of information. Most videos are too slow for intellectual curiosity. Clickbait, flamebait, and low-quality content.

In Comments

Be kind. Don't be snarky. Have curious conversations. When disagreeing, please reply to the argument instead of calling names. "That is idiotic" is not an argument.

Show K. Notes

Show K. Notes is for something you've made that other people can play with. Please don't submit blog posts, landing pages with only a signup form, or things that aren't functional.
