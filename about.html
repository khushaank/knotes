About K. Notes

What is K. Notes?

K. Notes is a community-driven news aggregator and discussion forum focused on technology, science, computer science, and "anything that gratifies one's intellectual curiosity."

Inspired by the minimalist and functional design of Hacker News, K. Notes aims to provide a clean, fast, and high-signal environment for developers, researchers, and lifelong learners to share and discuss the most interesting content on the web.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a community where curiosity is the primary driver of interaction. We value:

  • Intellectual Curiosity: Content that makes you think, learn, or see the world differently.
  • Civil Discourse: Respectful, substantive discussions that focus on ideas rather than individuals.
  • Signal over Noise: A focus on high-quality content over clickbait and sensationalism.

Technology

K. Notes is built using modern web technologies to ensure speed and reliability:

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML, Tailwind CSS for styling.
  • Backend: Supabase for database and authentication.
  • Design: A tribute to the classic Hacker News aesthetic with modern refinements.
